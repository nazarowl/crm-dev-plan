CRM Roadmap

Development Plan, Modules, and Task Breakdown

This page consolidates the roadmap from development-plan.md, milestones.md, and task-backlog.md into one browser-friendly view.

6 Delivery phases
6 Release milestones
13 Epics / modules
45 Implementation tasks

Planning Assumptions

  • apps/web and apps/api are scaffold-only starting points.
  • The audited production CRM is the main behavioral reference.
  • The paperwork defines the final Aqua Plus alignment target.
  • Out of scope: full e-commerce, inventory, loyalty, partner promo codes, and recommendation tooling.

Delivery Strategy

1. Foundation and domain alignment
2. Core CRM parity
3. Aqua Plus workflow alignment
4. Automations and notifications
5. Public booking and scheduling
6. Invoicing and payment workflow

Phases

Phase 1

Foundation and Domain Alignment

Objectives
  • Formalize backend stack and shared architecture.
  • Define domain entities and relationships.
  • Implement auth and permissions foundation.
  • Create shared contracts, migrations, and configuration.
Output

Stable technical foundation, permission-aware shell, and an initial database schema.

Phase 2

Core CRM Parity

Objectives
  • Rebuild the audited CRM baseline in the new codebase.
  • Preserve high-value operational behavior already in production.
Output

Usable internal CRM aligned closely enough to support migration planning.

Phase 3

Aqua Plus Workflow Alignment

Objectives
  • Align stages, categories, service taxonomy, and role naming.
  • Make lead origin visible in workflow and reporting.
Output

CRM language and workflow reflect the actual Aqua Plus operating model.

Phase 4

Automations and Notifications

Objectives
  • Move the CRM beyond passive record-keeping.
  • Add follow-ups, reminders, confirmations, and internal alerts.
Output

Automation-ready CRM with auditable notification behavior.

Phase 5

Public Booking and Scheduling Integration

Objectives
  • Connect customer booking to internal operations.
  • Respect availability rules and synchronize with the internal calendar.
Output

Synchronized booking and internal scheduling.

Phase 6

Invoicing and Payment Workflow

Objectives
  • Complete the commercial lifecycle beyond quotes.
  • Add invoice generation, payment schedules, statuses, and financial history.
Output

Quote-to-invoice-to-payment workflow inside the CRM.

Milestones

Milestone 1

Foundation

Real app structure replaces scaffold-only code. Auth, roles, permissions, shared types, and migrations are operational.

Milestone 2

Internal CRM MVP

Staff can use leads, contacts, pipeline, activities, calendar, quotes, and admin tools for daily work.

Milestone 3

Aqua Plus Alignment

Workflow language, service taxonomy, role profiles, and lead source tracking match the target business model.

Milestone 4

Automation Layer

Lead replies, internal alerts, reminders, and follow-up sequences become traceable system behavior.

Milestone 5

Booking Integration

Customer booking flows connect to staff calendars and internal CRM records.

Milestone 6

Billing Operations

Invoices, payment schedules, statuses, and customer financial history are supported natively.
